Christmas

The story behind Christmas:

The most well-known holiday around the world is Christmas. It is celebrated by Christians around the world as a means of celebrating the birth of Jesus Christ.

How Christmas is celebrated:

Christmas is celebrated on the 25th of December every year. Families will gather around a Christmas tree and give each other gifts. It is a time of celebration and warmth.

Other Christmas activities:

Another important element of Christmas is Santa Claus: a mythical man who visits the homes of children across the world on the night before Christmas and gives them a gift based on if they were "naughty or nice" that year.

Santa is aided in his efforts by his elves.

And is pulled in a sleigh by reindeer.
